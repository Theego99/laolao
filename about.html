About Laolao Travels

At Laolao, our mission is to fight overtourism by crafting high-quality, affordable experiences in Japan. We partner with local guides and small communities to bring you authentic journeys—far from the crowds.

  • Our Vision: Promote sustainable tourism that gives back to local communities, while ensuring travelers get the best possible experience for less.
  • Our Approach: We limit group sizes, choose eco-friendly accommodations, and avoid heavily trafficked spots—so you see the “real” Japan.
  • Core Values: Sustainability, Authenticity, Affordability, and Community Support.
