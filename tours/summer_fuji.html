Lake Tanuki & Fujisan Shrine

Full-day from Shinjuku: explore Lake Tanuki, then visit Fujisan Hongū Sengen Shrine.

  1. Meet at Shinjuku Station (West Exit)
    You’ll receive a personalized Suica transport card loaded with all fares.
  2. 07:00 Highway bus to Lake Tanuki area.
  3. 11:00–12:30 Stroll or rent a bike around Lake Tanuki; optional rowboat for the “double-diamond” Fuji view.
  4. 12:30–13:30 Lakeside lunch—try Fujinomiya yakisoba or café specialties.
  5. 13:35–14:30 Bus up to Fujisan Hongū Sengen Shrine (UNESCO World Heritage).
  6. 15:00 Train via Fuji Station back to Shinjuku (Shinkansen Kodama or JR).
  7. Arrive Shinjuku around 18:30.
