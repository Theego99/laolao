Cherry Blossoms & Countryside Cycling

Pedal through small towns beneath pink sakura canopies.

Experience rural Japan at its most beautiful, with local homestays and authentic cuisine. This 2-day tour includes:

  • Guided cycling beneath cherry blossom-covered roads
  • Traditional countryside homestay experience
  • Local farm-fresh meals each day
  • All gear rental (bicycle, helmet, etc.)

Day 1: Meet in Tokyo at 8 AM, bullet train to rural Yamanashi, cycle through villages, evening homestay.
Day 2: Early morning cycling under sakura canopies, return to Tokyo by 6 PM.
