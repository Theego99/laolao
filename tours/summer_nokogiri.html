Nokogiriyama & Cape Futtsu

Day trip from Shinagawa: climb Nokogiriyama’s cliff temples and stroll Cape Futtsu’s coastline.

  1. Meet at Shinagawa Station (East Exit)
    You’ll receive a pre-loaded Suica card for all transport today.
  2. 07:30 Keikyu Line to Kurihama.
  3. 08:15–09:00 Ferry to Kanaya pier.
  4. 09:00–10:30 Hike Nokogiriyama: see the Hyaku-shaku Kannon and the giant Buddha.
  5. 11:00 Picnic lunch with mountain vistas.
  6. 11:45–12:30 Train + bus to Cape Futtsu Park.
  7. 12:30–14:00 Beachside stroll and Mt. Fuji views.
  8. 14:30 Return to Shinagawa, arrive by early evening.

Quick Facts
  • Duration: 1 day
  • Group Size: Up to 10 people
  • Difficulty: Moderate (1 km climb)
  • Meeting Point: Shinagawa Station East Exit
  • Return: Shinagawa by evening
